Name of the game

Instructions:

  • You are playing as the rocket on the left side of the screen
  • You move the rocket up and down by pressing the UP arrow respectively the DOWN arrow
  • Your goal is to avoid the meteors coming at you and collect as many stars as you can
  • If you hit a meteor you lose the game and start over at the current level
  • After you've reached 10 stars you move onto the next level
